Question, Challenge, Define, State

CC Lecture

This week’s prompt begins to make sense of last week’s research. Begin shaping information into understanding. Use the following techniques to do this.
Define: Establish values driving opinion
Connect: Make constellations of research findings
Challenge: Assumptions, Black box thinking
Question: Inquire about what isn’t understood, what seems unexpected
State: Develop statement of opinion

Design:

Based on last week's research techniques and findings, I figured that flat hunting or house hunting in a new place is a big task. There are a lot of challenges and problems around the same.



Connect:

Similar to my observations, I realised a lot of people have had similar experiences while house hunting. During the time of our bootcamp, I interacted with some students and learnt that they were facing the same problem.



Challenge:

There are a couple of challenges that come in the path of solving this problem. One major issue is the fact that the images uploaded on these websites turn out to be fake or do not match the actual living space. This is creates a huge gap between the two parties. Another challenge is that many of these are fake listings and fraud ads. This becomes a hassle for the person to identify such cases. In some cases there is also the involvement of brokers. Clients have pay heavy brokerage as well which could also pose an issue.



Question:

Most of these websites claim to help find rooms and flats but it seems to be only a claim. They are using the same approach to solve the problem that has a couple of challenges which remain unsolved.
How is the client supposed to verify and trust these listings? There either needs to be an intervention or manual approval of such website posts.



State:

I believe the solve is not being solved efficiently. Can there be a better use of upcoming technologies apart from the already integrated 3D walk throughs? The approach needs to change because it is either the approach that is not well designed and well thought through or there still remains an unidentified hook to this problem.
