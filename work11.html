Major Studio I

Another thing that could be done for building community and where most of our youth spend time is on Youtube. Many people use youtube for uploading videos to their respective channels, follow YouTubers, or listening to music, watching music videos.

So, when I was searching for my apartment in new york city on google, There were some videos too. So, I saw some of them and realized many people had shared their experience/journey to get an apartment in new york.

From these videos as well, we get to know about the places to look for, websites that helped, etc.
Another Youtube channel I came across was of Erik Conover, who is a verified YouTuber and make vlogs of luxury apartments available in new york and other videos too.

So, I came up with the concept of having a Youtube channel, which helps people to look for apartments available. I can upload the videos of the apartment tour with proper description and person to contact. Here is the one video, I tried giving an example of :

Another thing that I thought of was What if people, who are going to different places to see the venue can make the tour videos and share with us, and I can upload them as well.
Because for example, If I am looking for a place, I will go and see 3-5 places or maybe more before finalizing one. So, similarly, if everyone who is looking for place visits and made tour videos and shares the ones that he/she is not interested in might interest another person!
By this way, you are not only going and looking for yourself, but helping others. And in the same way, a video by another person can help you!
