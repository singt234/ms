Major Studio I

Guiding Question

I was researching the topic; what else could be done? Or what else can I make to solve this problem? And by mistake, Siri gets active. So, I asked Siri, "Can you help me find an apartment in new york?" to see whether Siri can help me or not? Or what results Siri going to show me?

So, Siri showed me the name of the apartments that are there in new york! So, I thought maybe I am not asking Siri the right question. So, I asked Siri, " Can you help me find an apartment available to rent in new york?". This time Siri showed me a different result. But again, this was not helpful.

Concept

So, I came up with an idea of making a bot - a chatbot where you can ask questions related to the listings, rooms available, budget listings, etc.
I don't know whether this is feasible or not through technology. But it would be great if there's a chatbot who knows everything and can clear your doubts and help you in some way.

Prototype

So, I tried prototyping it with a friend (Akshay) who is searching for a room, and I am the bot. I will try to solve and answer his questions in the best possible manner; I would like the chatbot to answer.
