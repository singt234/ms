Major Studio I

Guiding Question

For this prototype, I was thinking about the brokers and agents who we connect with to give us the tour of the apartment. I was thinking about them. How can they show us all the places in one single place? Instead of going to different places, what if we can go to one location and there itself they can showcase all of them. It will save us all a lot of time!

Concept

For concept, I was thinking the use of Virtual Reality technology. With the use of this technology, agents/brokers can provide them the VR headsets and showcase the apartments and give them the tour their itself.
